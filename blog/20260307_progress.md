## Worked out impact of clientHold in DNS

Looking after `polonez.org.au`, worked out we had a `clientHold`, still not
100% sure what it is, but basically if our payment bounced, they hold and don't
return where the nameservers are. The unfortunate thing is that the registrar
just displayed **Active** so it was unclear that something was missing.

```sh
whois polonez.org.au | grep -C 1 -i 'status\|expiry\|renew'

status:       ACTIVE
remarks:      Registration information:
--
Reseller Name:
Status: clientHold https://identitydigital.au/whois-status-codes#clientHold
Registrant Contact ID: bc4ed7830c0740109510a594a897f60d-AU
```

After contacting support and paying the fee - hopefull this will be sorted.
